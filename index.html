Kickoff Your Hulu Party: A Guide to Shared Streaming

This extension is expertly designed to provide a flawless streaming experience. Besides, with only a few clicks, you can effortlessly enter the realm of synchronized streaming. Whether you're a newcomer to Hulu Party or an experienced viewer, this extension allows you to easily connect and enjoy your favorite shows and movies with friends and family, regardless of their location:

  • Install Hulu Watch Party First, you must install the Hulu Watch Party extension on your device. Note that this extension is only compatible with Chromebook, Windows, or macOS devices and works with Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge browsers. Further, click here to download and install the extension. Remember to pin Hulu Party to your toolbar for quick and easy access.
  • Pin Hulu Party to Your Toolbar After installing the Hulu Watch Party Chrome extension, ensure effortless access by pinning it to your toolbar. Moreover, click the “Extensions” icon and pin the HWP button to keep it handy for smooth and seamless use
  • Log in to Hulu To begin your shared streaming experience, log in to your Hulu account. This will unlock access to all of Hulu’s content—TV shows, series, movies, and more—allowing you to enjoy them together with others online.
  • Choose and Play Your Video Explore Hulu’s vast collection to pick the perfect show or movie for your watch party. After selecting, hit play to start the entertainment and enjoy a synchronized viewing experience with your friends and family
  • Launch a Watch Party With your video playing, click the “Extensions” icon in your toolbar to open the “Create a Party” window. Then, hit “Start the Party” to get your unique party URL. Please share this link with friends to invite them and start streaming together
  • Join a Hulu Party Firstly, log in to your Hulu account and click the party link your friend sent. However, if you still need to install the Hulu Watch Party extension, do so. Once it's installed, you'll be automatically directed to the watch party, where you can enjoy the show with everyone.
